LTT 公告 2026 年 2 月營收為 1.07 億元，年增 128.79%、月減 13.73%。累計 2026 年 1–2 月營收達 2.32 億元，年增 294.97%。

本次營收成長主要受惠於負極產品穩定出貨。LTT 持續優化產品結構並強化量產能力，以滿足市場對高效能材料的需求，推動公司長期穩健成長。

LTT 公告 2026 年 2 月营收为 1.07 亿元，同比增长 128.79%、环比减少 13.73%。累计 2026 年 1–2 月营收达 2.32 亿元，同比增长 294.97%。

本次营收增长主要得益于负极产品稳定出货。LTT 持续优化产品结构并强化量产能力，以满足市场对高性能材料的需求，推动公司长期稳健成长。

LTT announced that its February 2026 revenue reached NT$107 million (NT$107,405 thousand), representing a year-over-year increase of 128.79% and a month-over-month decrease of 13.73%. Cumulative revenue for the first two months of 2026 totaled NT$232 million (NT$231,904 thousand), up 294.97% year-over-year, reflecting strong operational growth and sustained momentum compared to the same period last year.

The growth is primarily attributed to the stable shipments of anode products. LTT continues to optimize its product mix and strengthen production capacity to meet market demand for high-performance materials, supporting the company's long-term sustainable growth.
