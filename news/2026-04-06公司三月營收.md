<!-- lang:tw -->

LTT 公告 2026 年 3 月營收為 1.61 億元，年增 102.31%、月增 49.59%。累計 2026 年第一季（1–3 月）營收達 3.93 億元，年增 184.20%。

本次營收成長主要受惠於負極產品穩定出貨。榮炭持續優化產品結構並強化量產能力，以滿足市場對高效能材料的需求，推動公司長期穩健成長。

<!-- lang:cn -->

LTT 公告 2026 年 3 月营收为新台币 1.61 亿元，同比增长 102.31%、环比增长 49.59%。累计 2026 年第一季度（1–3 月）营收达新台币 3.93 亿元，同比增长 184.20%。

本次营收增长主要得益于负极产品稳定出货。荣炭持续优化产品结构并强化量产能力，以满足市场对高性能材料的需求，推动公司长期稳健成长。

---

<!-- lang:en -->

LTT announced that its March 2026 revenue reached NT$161 million, representing a year-over-year increase of 102.31% and a month-over-month increase of 49.59%. Cumulative revenue for the first quarter of 2026 (January–March) totaled NT$393 million, up 184.20% year-over-year.

The growth is primarily attributed to the stable shipments of anode products. Rong Tan continues to optimize its product mix and strengthen production capacity to meet market demand for high-performance materials, supporting the company's long-term sustainable growth.
